About Lifehouse Power Chapel

Transforming Lives, Impacting Nations

Who We Are

Lifehouse Power Chapel is a Christ-centered ministry with a mission to raise Kingdom ambassadors who reflect the glory and authority of God in every sphere of life. Founded on the principles of faith, prayer, and love, we exist to empower individuals and families to live victoriously through the Word of God.

Our Mission

To win souls, disciple believers, and raise Kingdom-minded leaders equipped to influence their communities through faith, wisdom, and power.

Our Vision

To be a dynamic, spirit-filled church transforming lives and nations through the teaching of God’s Word, effective prayer, and outreach.

Our Story

What began as a small prayer gathering has grown into a vibrant family of believers committed to advancing God’s Kingdom. Over the years, Lifehouse Power Chapel has become a place of healing, empowerment, and divine encounters — a true house of power.

The lifehouse Power Chapel was stated in April 2003 with eleven (11) people by Bishop Samuel Acheampong with the support of Lady Pastor Helena Acheampong by the grace of God we have increased and still growing to the glory of God. Our mission is to “To win Souls, To Raise leaders and To plant Churches through the power of the Word of God “. Mathew 28:19-20; Our Vision “Developing Christlike Believers “. Ephesians 4:12-13; Through this mission and Vision many Souls have been won and equipped into the kingdom of God to the glory of God.

Meet Our Senior Pastor

Bishop Samuel Acheampong

A passionate teacher of the Word and a vessel of transformation, Bishop Samuel Acheampong has dedicated his life to guiding believers into deeper intimacy with Christ and empowering them to live purpose-driven lives.
